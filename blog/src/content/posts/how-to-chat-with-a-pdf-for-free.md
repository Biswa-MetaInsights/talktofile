## Getting better answers: how to prompt

Clearer questions produce better answers. Avoid vague prompts like "What is this about?" and instead scope your request:

- "Summarize the main arguments about the budget proposal in chapter three."
- "List the three policy recommendations and their page numbers."
- "Find all mentions of 'climate risk' and give the sentence and page for each."

Including page ranges, section names, or exact phrases helps the assistant locate the right passages in long documents. Always ask for page numbers or quoted text when you need citations.
